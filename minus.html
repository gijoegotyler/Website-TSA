Disadvantages of Cloud Computing

Price

Although you save money by not needing a powerful computer, you need to pay for the service of it being hosted on a server and you might need to upgrade your internet because it requires high speed internet. This is good in the short term but long term investing in a nice computer and security software is cheaper.

Security

Security is a risk, but it also has some advantages. There is the advantage of if your computer is stolen you are safe. However, major cloud services, especially stoarge, are big targets for hackers. It is very good for a hacker to attack a cloud storage company because they can steal a lot of info at once.

Dependencies

The dependencies of cloud computing are a major negative. Cloud Computing requires a powerful internet connection to be able to connect to apps and services. This causes issues because you cannot use some of your apps and services where you don't have internet.
