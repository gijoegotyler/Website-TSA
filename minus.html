Disadvantages of Cloud Computing

Cost

Although you save money by not needing a powerful computer, you need to pay for the service of it being hosted on a server, and you might need to upgrade your internet because it requires high speed internet. This is good in the short term, but thinking long term, investing in a nice computer and security software is cheaper.

Security

Major cloud services, especially stoarge, are big targets for hackers. It is most profitable for a hacker to target an attack at a cloud storage company because they can steal a lot of information at once.

Dependencies

The dependencies of cloud computing are a major negative. Cloud Computing requires a powerful internet connection to be able to connect to apps and services. This causes issues because you cannot use some of your apps and services when you don't have an internet connection.
